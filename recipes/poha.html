Poha - Recipe


Poha is a light and fluffy flattened rice dish, cooked with onions, spices, and a hint of lemon juice for a tangy kick.

Ingredients

  • Flattened rice (poha)
  • onions
  • green chilies
  • curry leaves
  • mustard seeds
  • turmeric
  • peanuts
  • coriander
  • lemon
  • sugar
  • salt

Steps

  1. Prepare the poha: Rinse poha under running water, drain, and keep aside.
  2. Cook the base: Heat oil, splutter mustard seeds, and saut√© onions, curry leaves, and chilies. Add turmeric.
  3. Combine: Mix in poha, peanuts, sugar, and salt. Cook for a few minutes.
  4. Garnish: Finish with lemon juice and coriander leaves before serving.
