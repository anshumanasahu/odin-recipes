Samosa - Recipe


A crispy and golden triangular pastry stuffed with a savory spiced potato filling, Samosa is a popular Indian snack enjoyed with chutney or tea.

Ingredients

    For the Dough

  • All-purpose flour (maida)
  • carom seeds
  • salt
  • water
  • oil/ghee

    • For the filling

  • Potatoes
  • peas
  • green chilies
  • ginger
  • cumin seeds
  • coriander powder
  • garam masala
  • red chili powder
  • amchur (dry mango powder)
  • salt

Steps

  1. Prepare the dough: Mix flour, salt, and carom seeds. Add oil/ghee and rub into the flour. Gradually add water to form a stiff dough. Rest it for 30 minutes.
  2. Make the filling: Boil and mash the potatoes. Saut√© cumin seeds, green chilies, and ginger in oil, then add spices, mashed potatoes, and peas. Cook until well combined.
  3. Assemble: Roll the dough into circles, cut into halves, and shape into cones. Fill with the prepared stuffing and seal the edges.
  4. Fry: Deep-fry in medium-hot oil until golden and crispy.
